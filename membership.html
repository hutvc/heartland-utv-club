Membership

$125 Per Year

Membership is good for one full year from the day you sign up.

Household Membership

Includes 2 adults and anyone under 18 in the household.

24/7 Access

Full access to the 200 acre property including 130 acres of timber trails.

One Machine Included

1 machine on the trails included with membership. Additional machines can be on the trails with a day pass or second machine yearly pass.

Member Benefits

  • Members shop / clubhouse
  • Indoor restrooms
  • Machine wash down area
  • Fishing pond
  • Discounts on Quakerdale camping and venue

Guest Machine Day Passes

Day passes are available for guest machines. If you have fun and decide to join, your day pass goes toward membership if purchased the same day.
